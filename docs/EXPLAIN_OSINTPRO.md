# How To Explain OSINTPRO

Use this page when presenting OSINTPRO to users, agencies, consultants, GitHub visitors or potential customers.

## One-Sentence Version

OSINTPRO turns passive public evidence from domains, usernames and blockchain wallets into client-ready investigation graphs, reports and monitoring.

## 30-Second Version

OSINTPRO is a passive OSINT workspace for consultants, small agencies and fraud analysts. It checks public domain, email, web, social and wallet signals, converts them into readable findings, connects the evidence in an entity graph and exports reports for client delivery.

It is not an exploit scanner. It is designed for authorized public-source intelligence, monitoring and reporting.

## 2-Minute Demo Script

1. Open the live demo.
2. Register with a nickname and password.
3. Run `Domain Intel` on a public domain you own or are allowed to review.
4. Show the score, DNS, email posture, headers, CT names and findings.
5. Open `Entity Graph` and show how the domain, technologies, findings and related signals connect.
6. Open `Web Audit Lab` and show beginner-friendly explanations and safe commands.
7. Open `Wallet Trace` with a public wallet address and show public movement, counterparties and case notes.
8. Open `Billing` and explain why monitoring, exports and client workflows are paid features.

## What Makes It Different

OSINTPRO is not trying to win by collecting the most raw data. It wins by making public evidence easier to understand and deliver.

Core differentiators:

- client-ready investigation graph
- plain-English findings
- passive-only safety boundary
- beginner-friendly Web Audit Lab
- wallet tracing for public fraud reconstruction
- account-isolated history and client folders
- PDF/CSV exports for agencies
- monitoring for recurring client value
