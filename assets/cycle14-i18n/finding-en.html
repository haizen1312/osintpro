OSINTPRO i18n proof

lowCAA missing

The domain does not publicly restrict which CAs can issue certificates.

How an attacker may abuse it: If a certificate authority or validation process is abused, there is no DNS-level policy limiting issuance.

Business impact: Weaker certificate governance and harder incident scoping after suspicious issuance.

Owner action: Add CAA records for approved CAs and document who can change certificate policy.

Evidence to collect: Collect current CAs, wildcard usage, ACME providers and certificate issuance history.
